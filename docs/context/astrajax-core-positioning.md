# AstraJax Core Positioning Context Pack

**Status:** Bootstrap draft.  
**Primary destination:** Cursor/GitHub.  
**Owner:** Matthew.  
**Primary sources:** `astrajax_positioning.md`, `astrajax_ops_brief.md`, `AGENTS.md`.

## Purpose

Give agents a concise, source-linked pack for AstraJax positioning, founder proof,
claim-control, and sensitive-context boundaries.

## Canonical Claims

- AstraJax helps commercial teams turn domain expertise, messy workflows, and
  scattered data into AI-ready operating systems.
- The hard part of useful AI is not only tools. It is clean data, clear
  workflows, adoption, bounded agents, and human judgement.
- Matthew is a non-technical commercial leader who has never handwritten a line
  of code and has built production software entirely with AI on top of clean
  operational data.
- The Butternut Box Direct Sales story is the canonical proof point for the
  AstraJax thesis.
- Clive is the managed context environment for teams using AI agents or reaching
  that maturity through AstraJax.

## Claim-Control Rules

- Pair "never written a line of code" with "with AI, on top of clean data."
- Pair "built fast" with the preceding foundation year of data cleaning and
  system architecture.
- Pair "AI agents" with bounded scope, human approval, and audit trail.
- Do not present Matthew as an engineer.
- Do not imply every team can skip technical expertise forever.
- Do not include personal finances or medical specifics in shareable context.

## Preferred Framing

Use:

```text
AstraJax structures the work. Clive structures the context. Agents use both.
```

Avoid:

- generic AI consulting
- "Matthew builds Airtable bases"
- broad pet-business or lead-gen drift
- overclaiming technical novelty
